About Global Express Shipping

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Global Express Shipping started as a small local courier service and has grown into an international logistics company serving clients in over 200 countries. Our commitment to reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for businesses and individuals alike.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional shipping services that connect businesses and people around the world, with a focus on reliability, speed, and outstanding customer service.

Our Values

  • Reliability: We deliver on our promises every time.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our processes and technology.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to reducing our environmental impact.
  • Customer Focus: We prioritize customer needs in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency.

Our Global Network

With offices in 45 countries and partnerships with local carriers worldwide, we offer seamless shipping solutions across the globe. Our network includes:

  • 150+ distribution centers
  • 5,000+ delivery vehicles
  • 25+ cargo aircraft
  • Partnerships with major shipping lines
